The Events section shows Linux Foundation events relevant to you and your projects. You can browse upcoming events, view event details, and manage your attendance.

## Your personal events

The information below reflects **your individual** event registrations and attendance — events you are registered for or that are relevant to your project affiliations.

### What you can do

- Browse upcoming and past LFX events
- View event details including dates, location, and description
- The Events page shows events you are already registered for. Registration is typically handled on the event's external registration page.
- Filter using the **All Foundations**, **All Roles**, and **All Statuses** dropdowns. Switch between **Upcoming**, **Past**, **Visa Letters**, and **Travel Funding** tabs.

### Who this applies to

All authenticated users can browse events. Event visibility may vary based on your project affiliations and the active lens.

### Navigation

Go to **app.lfx.dev** and select **Events** from the left navigation sidebar. The events page lists upcoming events relevant to your projects and profile.

## Your company's activity

The **Events** page in Org Lens (route: `/org/events`) shows Linux Foundation events in the context of your organization — including events your company is sponsoring, events where your organization has a significant presence, and events attended by members of your organization.

### What you can see

- Events your organization is sponsoring or participating in at an organizational level
- Attendance data for members affiliated with your company
- Upcoming events relevant to your organization's areas of interest

### Navigation

Switch to the **Org** lens using the lens selector at the top of LFX Self Serve, then select **Events** from the left navigation sidebar (route: `/org/events`).

### Who can access this

Viewing your organization's events in Org Lens requires **admin access** to your organization in LFX. If you are not yet an admin, another admin at your organization can invite you to accept an admin role. Once you accept, you will be able to access the Org lens events page for your company.
